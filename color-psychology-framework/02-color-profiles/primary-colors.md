# Primary Colors: Red, Blue, Yellow

## Red (#FF0000)

### Psychology & Meaning
Red is the most emotionally intense color. It stimulates the heart rate, increases respiration, and raises blood pressure. Red demands attention and evokes strong emotions.

### Associations
| Positive | Negative |
|----------|----------|
| Passion, love, desire | Anger, aggression, danger |
| Energy, excitement | Violence, warning |
| Power, strength | Impulsiveness |
| Confidence, courage | Dominance, intimidation |

### Best Uses in Branding
- **Food & Beverage** — Stimulates appetite (McDonald's, Coca-Cola, KFC)
- **Entertainment** — Creates excitement (Netflix, YouTube)
- **Sales & Retail** — Drives urgency (Target, sale signs)
- **Automotive** — Conveys speed and power (Ferrari, Tesla accents)

### When to Avoid
- Healthcare (except emergency services)
- Financial services (associated with losses)
- Calming or wellness brands
- Overuse in any context (causes fatigue)

### Shades & Their Uses
| Shade | Hex | Best For |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Bright Red | #FF0000 | CTAs, urgency, sales |
| Crimson | #DC143C | Luxury, fashion |
| Burgundy | #800020 | Sophistication, wine, maturity |
| Coral | #FF7F50 | Feminine, warm, approachable |

## Blue (#0000FF)

### Psychology & Meaning
Blue is the most universally favored color. It has a calming effect, lowering heart rate and blood pressure. Blue conveys trust, reliability, and professionalism.

### Associations
| Positive | Negative |
|----------|----------|
| Trust, loyalty, dependability | Coldness, aloofness |
| Calm, peace, serenity | Sadness, depression |
| Intelligence, wisdom | Distance, conservatism |
| Professionalism, stability | Predictability |

### Best Uses in Branding
- **Technology** — Trust and innovation (IBM, Dell, Intel, Facebook)
- **Finance** — Stability and security (Chase, PayPal, Visa)
- **Healthcare** — Calm and trust (Pfizer, Oral-B)
- **Corporate** — Professionalism (LinkedIn, Ford)

### When to Avoid
- Food brands (suppresses appetite)
- Urgency-driven marketing
- Brands seeking warmth or excitement
- Children's products (unless combined with brighter colors)

### Shades & Their Uses
| Shade | Hex | Best For |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Navy | #000080 | Corporate, luxury, authority |
| Royal Blue | #4169E1 | Professional, confident |
| Sky Blue | #87CEEB | Friendly, approachable, calm |
| Teal | #008080 | Unique, creative, sophisticated |

## Yellow (#FFFF00)

### Psychology & Meaning
Yellow is the brightest and most energizing color. It stimulates mental activity, enhances concentration, and promotes optimism. However, overuse can cause anxiety.

### Associations
| Positive | Negative |
|----------|----------|
| Happiness, optimism, joy | Anxiety, cowardice |
| Creativity, intellect | Caution, warning |
| Energy, warmth | Impulsiveness |
| Attention-grabbing | Overwhelming (if overused) |

### Best Uses in Branding
- **Children's Products** — Fun and playful (LEGO)
- **Creative Industries** — Innovation (Snapchat, Post-it)
- **Food & Beverage** — Cheerful, warm (McDonald's accents, Subway)
- **Retail** — Attention-grabbing (Best Buy, IKEA)

### When to Avoid
- Luxury brands (can appear cheap)
- Financial services (associated with caution)
- Overuse anywhere (causes eye strain)
- Serious or somber contexts

### Shades & Their Uses
| Shade | Hex | Best For |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Bright Yellow | #FFFF00 | Attention, CTAs, warnings |
| Gold | #FFD700 | Luxury, achievement, premium |
| Mustard | #FFDB58 | Earthy, vintage, sophisticated |
| Cream | #FFFDD0 | Elegant, soft, understated |

## LLM-ACTIONABLE-INSIGHTS

### Quick Decision Guide for Primary Colors

**Choose RED when:**
- Brand needs to convey urgency, passion, or excitement
- Target audience responds to bold, confident messaging
- Industry is food, entertainment, or sports
- Goal is to stand out and demand attention

**Choose BLUE when:**
- Trust and credibility are paramount
- Industry is tech, finance, or healthcare
- Audience values stability and professionalism
- Brand personality is calm, reliable, authoritative

**Choose YELLOW when:**
- Brand is optimistic, creative, or youthful
- Need to grab attention without aggression
- Target audience is children or creative professionals
- Industry values innovation and energy

### Color Combination Suggestions
- **Red + Blue** — Bold yet trustworthy
- **Red + Yellow** — Energetic and attention-grabbing
- **Blue + Yellow** — Professional yet approachable
- **Blue + White** — Clean, trustworthy, tech-focused
