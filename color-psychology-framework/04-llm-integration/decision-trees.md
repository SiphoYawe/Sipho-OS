# Color Psychology Decision Trees

## Master Decision Tree: Brand Color Selection

```
START: What is the primary emotion you want your brand to evoke?

├── TRUST / RELIABILITY
│   ├── Industry: Finance, Healthcare, Tech?
│   │   └── YES → Blue (primary) + White/Gray (secondary)
│   └── Want warmth too?
│       └── YES → Blue + Green or Blue + Warm accent
│
├── EXCITEMENT / ENERGY
│   ├── Food or Entertainment industry?
│   │   └── YES → Red + Yellow/Orange
│   ├── Sports or Fitness?
│   │   └── YES → Red + Black or Orange + Black
│   └── General energy/action?
│       └── Orange + Blue (complementary contrast)
│
├── LUXURY / PREMIUM
│   ├── Traditional luxury?
│   │   └── YES → Black + Gold
│   ├── Creative luxury?
│   │   └── YES → Purple + Gold or Black + Deep Red
│   └── Modern minimalist luxury?
│       └── Black + White (with minimal accent)
│
├── CREATIVITY / INNOVATION
│   ├── Tech/Digital focus?
│   │   └── YES → Purple + Blue or Teal + Orange
│   ├── Arts/Design focus?
│   │   └── YES → Purple + Orange or Bold unique combinations
│   └── Children/Playful?
│       └── Bright primaries (Red, Blue, Yellow)
│
├── NATURE / HEALTH / SUSTAINABILITY
│   ├── Organic/Natural products?
│   │   └── YES → Green + Brown + Cream
│   ├── Wellness/Mindfulness?
│   │   └── YES → Green + Blue or Lavender
│   └── Environmental/Eco?
│       └── Green + Earth tones + White
│
├── FRIENDLINESS / APPROACHABILITY
│   ├── Youthful audience?
│   │   └── YES → Orange + Blue or Yellow + Blue
│   ├── Warm/Cozy feeling?
│   │   └── YES → Orange + Brown + Cream
│   └── Casual/Relaxed?
│       └── Light Blue + Warm accent
│
└── SOPHISTICATION / PROFESSIONALISM
    ├── Corporate/Traditional?
    │   └── Navy + Gray + White
    ├── Modern/Contemporary?
    │   └── Charcoal + White + Bold accent
    └── Creative Professional?
        └── Navy + Orange or Gray + Teal
```

## Decision Tree: Industry-Specific Color Selection

```
What industry is the brand in?

├── TECHNOLOGY / SOFTWARE
│   ├── B2B / Enterprise?
│   │   └── Blue + Gray + White (trust, professionalism)
│   ├── Consumer / Social?
│   │   └── Blue + Friendly accent (approachable)
│   └── AI / Innovation focus?
│       └── Purple + Blue or Gradient (creative tech)
│
├── FINANCE / BANKING
│   ├── Traditional bank?
│   │   └── Blue + Green + Gold accents
│   ├── Fintech / Startup?
│   │   └── Brighter blue or Green + Modern accent
│   └── Investment / Premium?
│       └── Navy + Gold + Black
│
├── HEALTHCARE / WELLNESS
│   ├── Hospital / Clinical?
│   │   └── Blue + White + Soft green
│   ├── Alternative / Holistic?
│   │   └── Green + Purple + Soft tones
│   └── Fitness / Energy?
│       └── Orange + Black or Red + Black
│
├── FOOD & BEVERAGE
│   ├── Fast Food / Quick Service?
│   │   └── Red + Yellow + White
│   ├── Healthy / Organic?
│   │   └── Green + Brown + Cream
│   ├── Premium / Fine Dining?
│   │   └── Black + Gold or Burgundy + Cream
│   └── Coffee / Café?
│       └── Brown + Cream + Warm accent
│
├── RETAIL / E-COMMERCE
│   ├── Budget / Value?
│   │   └── Bright colors, often Yellow + Red accents
│   ├── Mid-market?
│   │   └── Blue or Green + Neutral
│   └── Premium / Luxury?
│       └── Black + Gold or Minimal palette
│
├── BEAUTY / FASHION
│   ├── Mass market?
│   │   └── Pink + Bold accents
│   ├── Premium?
│   │   └── Black + Gold or Sophisticated palette
│   └── Natural / Clean beauty?
│       └── Green + Nude tones + White
│
└── PROFESSIONAL SERVICES
    ├── Legal / Accounting?
    │   └── Navy + Gray + Minimal accent
    ├── Consulting?
    │   └── Blue + Professional accent
    └── Creative Agency?
        └── Bold, unique combinations (differentiation)
```

## Decision Tree: Color for Specific Applications

```
What are you designing?

├── WEBSITE / APP
│   ├── What's the primary action?
│   │   ├── Sign up / Purchase → Orange or Green CTA
│   │   ├── Learn more → Blue CTA
│   │   └── Urgent action → Red CTA
│   └── What's the mood?
│       ├── Professional → Blue/Gray/White
│       ├── Friendly → Warm colors + White
│       └── Modern → Dark mode or High contrast
│
├── LOGO
│   ├── Need to stand out?
│   │   └── Use unexpected color for industry
│   ├── Need to fit in?
│   │   └── Use industry-standard colors
│   └── Need versatility?
│       └── Simple palette (1-2 colors + black/white)
│
├── PACKAGING
│   ├── Premium product?
│   │   └── Black + Gold or Minimal white + accent
│   ├── Natural product?
│   │   └── Earth tones + Green + Kraft paper tones
│   ├── Youth product?
│   │   └── Bright, bold colors
│   └── Shelf standout?
│       └── Contrast with competitors
│
├── MARKETING MATERIALS
│   ├── Brand awareness?
│   │   └── Strong brand colors, consistent
│   ├── Call to action?
│   │   └── Contrasting accent color
│   └── Information-heavy?
│       └── Neutral backgrounds, color for hierarchy
│
└── SOCIAL MEDIA
    ├── Brand consistency?
    │   └── Use brand palette consistently
    ├── Engagement focus?
    │   └── Bright, saturated colors
    └── Professional tone?
        └── Muted, sophisticated palette
```

## LLM Conversation Flow

### Opening Assessment

```
STEP 1: Gather Context
"Tell me about your brand/project. What industry are you in?"
"Who is your target audience?"
"What are 3 words that describe your brand personality?"

STEP 2: Understand Goals
"What emotion do you want people to feel?"
"Do you want to blend in with your industry or stand out?"
"Are there any colors you definitely want to use or avoid?"

STEP 3: Provide Recommendation
Based on answers, use decision trees above to recommend:
- Primary color with reasoning
- 1-2 secondary/accent colors
- Example brands using similar palettes

STEP 4: Validate & Refine
"Does this feel right for your brand?"
"Any adjustments needed?"
"Where will you primarily use these colors?"
```

### Quick Recommendation Format

```
Based on your brand's [personality traits] in the [industry] sector,
targeting [audience], I recommend:

PRIMARY: [Color] — [Why it works]
SECONDARY: [Color] — [What it adds]
ACCENT: [Color] — [How to use it]

This palette conveys [emotion] while [differentiation point].
Similar successful brands: [Examples]
```
